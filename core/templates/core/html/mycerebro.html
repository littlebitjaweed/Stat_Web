What You Can Do on Upstra Partner

Upstra Partner is built for those who lead — coaches, scouts, and recruiters. Track your players, assess performance, and connect with top talent, all in one place.

  • Team Insights: Access live player stats, attendance, and progress logs.
  • Scouting Tools: Discover and follow top-performing athletes across clubs and regions.
  • Recruiter's Dashboard: Build shortlists, analyze performance trends, and manage outreach.

Live Feed & Talent Updates

Stay up-to-date with real-time updates from your teams and prospects. Review performance highlights, game videos, and training sessions — all personalized to your role.

  • Athlete Watchlist: Get notified about standout moments and player improvements.
  • Reports: Export match-day reports, fitness logs, and player assessments.
  • Feedback Loop: Leave reviews, suggestions, or progress notes for athletes in your club.
