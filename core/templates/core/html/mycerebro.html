What You Can Do on Upstra Partner

We've built Upstra to be more than just an app on your phone—it's your personal everyday tool, your digital locker room, and your connection to the entire sports world. Here's a closer look at what you can do:

  • Club Connect: Real-time updates from your club so you're always in the loop.
